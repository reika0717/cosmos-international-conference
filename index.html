Conference theme

Sustainable Trans-Equatorial Microalgae Technologies for the Advancement of Circular Economy

Scope

New algal (microalgae) eco-engineering technologies and applications are rapidly emerging in the field of aquaculture, nutraceuticals, cosmetics, medicals, wastewater treatment and more. Due to their ecological friendliness and alignment with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), these technologies are key for developing a recycling-oriented, sustainable civilization. Equatorial regions, in particular, are rich in natural solar energy throughout the year, and offer great potential of algal (microalgae) technologies and applications. The mission of this conference is to bridge and exchange knowledge and expertise of algal (microalgae) technologies, ranging from basic biological/ecological studies to social applications, towards a new era of eco-engineering.
